Frame these as expertise and background, never as a service offering.

## 3. WHO MY CUSTOMERS ARE
I don't have one narrow type yet — I'm still growing and figuring out who my best fit is. Right now my customers include:
- Homeowners in Arcata who want garden help but don't have the time or know-how
- Eco-conscious folks who want organic raised beds and edible gardens
- Older people or landlords who can't do the physical work themselves
- Anyone in Humboldt County who sees my Craigslist ad and needs yard work

The common thread: they're local, they care about their outdoor space, and they want someone they can trust to show up and do good work.
